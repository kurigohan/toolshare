About Toolshare

We are a web application that will allow users within a Share Zone to borrow physical tools from a shared location under the supervision of admins and shed owners/coordinators. The application will feature an easy-to-use intuitive interface for navigation. The purpose of this tool is to provide members of communities with an easy mechanism to share tools with each other.
